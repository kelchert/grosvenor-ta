Our Community Guidelines

  1. Focus on the positive
    • Share helpful information, resources, and opportunities
    • Celebrate our community's strengths and each other's contributions
    • Suggest solutions when identifying challenges
  2. Respect our shared space
    • Keep conversations constructive and considerate
    • Remember that neighbors of all backgrounds read these posts
    • Give others the benefit of the doubt in all interactions
  3. Keep it practical and relevant
    • Posts should relate to our building community or immediate neighborhood
    • Share information that benefits fellow residents
    • Use direct messages for conversations relevant to only a few people
  4. Protect privacy and safety
    • Don't share other residents' personal information without permission
    • Avoid posting sensitive building security information publicly
    • Use discretion when posting about your own comings and goings
  5. Building-related concerns
    • This community space is for neighbor-to-neighbor connection
    • Direct maintenance requests and management concerns through official channels
    • Focus discussions on solutions rather than complaints

Privacy Settings

Control what information other tenants can see about you.

Deactivate Account

If you're moving out or no longer wish to participate in the community, you can deactivate your account.

  • You will be removed from the tenant directory
  • You will no longer be able to log in
  • Your past messages and posts will remain but show as "Former Tenant"
  • This action cannot be undone by you (contact admin to reactivate)
